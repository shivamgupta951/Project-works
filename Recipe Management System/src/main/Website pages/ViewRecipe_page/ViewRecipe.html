Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Recipe Process:

Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans. Stir sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla; mix for 2 minutes on medium speed with an electric mixer. Stir in boiling water. The batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans. Bake until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 30–35 minutes. Cool and enjoy!
